Privacy Policy for RetroHome Alexa Skill

Last updated: January 8, 2026

The RetroHome Alexa Skill ("the Skill") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

The Skill uses account linking to authenticate users with your existing RetroHome account. When you link your account, we access the following information from your RetroHome account:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • User identifier/access token

This is the same information you provided when you created your RetroHome account through our official app.

How We Use Information

The collected information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate and verify your identity
  • Link your Alexa requests to your RetroHome account
  • Provide personalized smart home functionality
  • Process and fulfill your voice commands correctly

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your information is shared only with Amazon's Alexa service as necessary to provide the Skill's functionality. Amazon's use of your information is governed by the Amazon Privacy Notice.

Data Storage and Security

Any stored data is kept securely and is used only for the operation of the Skill.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. You can also unlink the Skill from your Alexa app at any time, which will revoke the Skill's access to your RetroHome account information.

To completely delete your RetroHome account and all associated data, please contact us.
